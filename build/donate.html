How your donation helps



Teen Journey is lean community organization powered by loyal volunteers who pour their heart and gifts into keeping the childrens fire alive. But it’s not easy, so here are two vital ways your contribution can help.



Sponsor a teen to make their journey into adulthood the journey of a lifetime

With your help, we can offer financial aid to help teens and families be able to afford our camps.



Keep the spirit of the village alive

It takes a village to raise a child. Your support can help us strengthen our support network for teens after the camps through monthly events, mentorship opportunities, and holistic health programs.



Teen Journey is also developing an online/offline mentorship model that aims to:
Offer year-round support for teens and parents as they integrate and implement their journey
Create a rich network of accessible, affordable, and confidential resources, tools, and training
Raise consciousness around the importance of eldership and mentorship
Help create a sustainable model for elders and mentors to offer their services
Build inspiring, lasting relationships and deepen the Teen Journey community
