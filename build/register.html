Registration policies



Registration ends on the day before the program start date, unless it is fully booked.



Payments



All deposits are due upon registration. Final balances are due 30 days before the program start date for local programs, and 60 days before the start date of international programs.



Refunds



All deposits are due upon registration. Final balances are due 30 days before the program start date for local programs, and 60 days before the start date of international programs.

Local programs: You can receive a 50% refund on your deposit 30 days or more before the program start date. No refunds are issued on deposits and final balances less than 30 days before the program start date.

International programs: You can receive a 50% refund on your deposit 60 days or more before the program start date. No refunds are issued on deposits and final balances less than 60 days before the program start date.
