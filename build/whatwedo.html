Teen Journey Summer Camp

July 26 – August 4, 2014
Ruby Lake Resort, Sunshine Coast, BC


Our annual 10-day summer camp is the heart of our work and vision. At this camp, we translate our vision and mission into an experience that makes a lasting positive impact on teens. Our programming is based on three areas:

  • NATURE

  • CULTURE

  • LIFEFORCE

Nature

Excursions in nature, small-scale building projects develop survival skills and knowledge of the natural world. There's also traditional practices of the vision quest and sweatlodge.



Culture

Music, dance, fine arts, and communication harness creative energy and awaken new capacities for connecting with oneself and others.



Life force

Daily yoga, meditation, and qigong practices build a sense of personal discipline.



The theme for this year’s camp is exploring personal destiny and co-creating the village. We will use the story of “The Alchemist” by Paulo Coelho - the most translated book in the world - as a framework to guide the journey as we empower them with the tools to live a life of purpose and adventure.

Parent Journey Intensive

August 2 – 4, 2014
Ruby Lake Resort, Sunshine Coast, BC


In this 2½-day intensive, parents will explore many of the same tools that their children will in Teen Journey. By awakening to what teens are going through, parents can more effectively and lovingly support their teens on their journey into adulthood.

This intensive will empower parents with the tools to not only effectively communicate with their teens, but also work through some of the challenges from their teenage years that unconsciously impact their relationships with their children today.

International trips



For our teens to become global citizens, they must expand their identity to include ideas and experiences from other cultures. While this can happen through school, the internet, and meeting people from different places, nothing replaces the power of travelling through foreign lands with a deep purpose.

Our international trips give youth the chance to experience the world with great depth and engagement through the lens of Teen Journey’s philosophy. These are no typical group travel experiences! In 2012, we visited a monastery in India to receive the teachings of the Dalai Lama, and in 2013, we trekked through ancient sites and mountains of Peru.



2012 Journey to India

Teen Journey and Inkarri took teens on an eye-opening and soul-inspiring trip to the sacred east, where we sat at the feet of the Dalai Lama and Tibetan monks in northern India. We received firsthand teachings of spiritual knowledge from the masters and explored vibrant cultures within and beyond the monastery walls.

View photos from our India trip on Flickr



2013 Journey to Peru

Teens and family travelled on this remarkable journey as an “ayllu” – a Quechua word for the big family, the community that works together, while also being in service to one another and to the larger whole. We encountered the ancient Andean healing path of a curandero (traditional healer), took an intimate pilgrimage to sacred sites and temples in Cusco, the Sacred Valley, mystical Machu Picchu, and the Amazon rainforest. Our guides shared ancient Andean cultural history and wisdom, and taught us how to connect with Pachamama (Mother Earth) and the Apus (Mountain Spirits).

Ongoing programs



It takes a village to raise a child. Through our extended network of leaders, mentors, and community members, we offer monthly events and programs throughout the year to deepen and reinforce the journey that began at summer camp. Many Teen Journey leaders make themselves available to teens following the camp for ongoing mentorship and support.

Reconnect with community, integrate your Teen Journey epiphanies, and continue building on your journey of self-discovery.

Training the Teen Journey model



Teen Journey is a model for those who seek to incorporate the practices of heart-based rites of passages into their own communities. We have created a structure that allows for communities to bring their own creative ideas and mentorship resources into a program for youth.

If you would like to bring the vision of Teen Journey to your community, we help you lay the foundations for catalyzing this amazing work – without reinventing the wheel. Please contact us to explore this opportunity. We look forward to hearing from you as co-creators of an emerging global village, connected from the heart.
