Theme: Exploring personal destiny and co-creating the village



“When you really want something to happen, the whole world conspires to help you achieve it.” - Paulo Coelho

For teens to seek personal meaning in their lives, they need to explore burning questions like “Who am I?” and “Why am I here?”. Our camp supports teens in this exploration of personal identity and connects it to a larger story of destiny that the world calls them to fulfill.

To guide this exploration, we will reference the popular story of The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho about a young boy Santiago who leaves home to follow his dream and fulfill his personal legend. Teens will leave camp feeling empowered with tools – just like Santiago – to live a life of purpose and adventure by following the calling of their hearts.

Unlike a traditional camp, we use a village model to offer teens a different vision of individual and social potential. It’s a place of belonging where youth, mentors, and leaders form a colorful fabric animated by the sincere, authentic expression of their gifts and hearts. Everyone is seen for who they are.

Why it matters

Activities that awaken the journey



Our activities open teens up to who they are and what it feels like to be the person they want to share with the world. To do this, our activities help them get in touch with the wisdom of their bodies, the gifts of the natural world, the power of their minds, and the fire in their hearts.

Activities fall into three categories: nature, culture and life-force. Teens learn survival skills and partner with nature through outdoor excursions and small-scale building projects. They awaken new capacities for connecting with creative energy with music, dance, and communication workshops. Finally, they practice daily yoga, meditation, and qigong to develop personal discipline.

Our programming helps teens tap into and synthesize multiple ways of knowing into a vibrant, ongoing journey of discovery – discovery of the self, others, and the world around them.

Camp Location



Ruby Lake Resort is located on the beautiful Sunshine Coast of British Columbia. Nestled into the Caren Mountain Range, this land has access to some of the oldest forest in BC – with trees up to 3,500 years old! This amazing resort is home to the Iris Griffith Wildlife Interpretive Nature Center that stewards 17 acres of pristine nature reserve with hundreds of plant and animal species.

The lake is the warmest on the coast and a perfect place for canoeing and water sports. Ruby Lake is also home to the largest natural amphitheater on the Sunshine Coast, a 40-foot geodesic dome that becomes an immersive media and educational environment, and the Spirit of the Rainforest Retreat Centre.

The Retreat Centre is run by a collective of artists, healers, and entrepreneurs from Vancouver and the Sunshine Coast who are committed to making a destination for individuals, families, and communities to learn, heal, create, and communicate through artistic productions, human-potential programs and celebratory gatherings.

Accommodation



Youth at Teen Journey work together with our team to co-create a temporary village made from tents and other structures on the beautiful lands of Ruby Lake Resort. It’s an exercise in creativity, collaboration, and self-reliance that exposes teens to simple living – putting available resources to use in practical and innovative ways – practised by many people around the world.

Parent Journey



Parents play such a vital role in Teen Journey that we created a 2½-day intensive to explore many of the same tools as their teens in Teen Journey. Parent Journey is included in the cost of Teen Journey and at least one parent or guardian must attend.

By awakening to what teens go through and gaining more effective communication tools, parents can more effectively and lovingly support them on their journey into adulthood. The combined experiences of Teen Journey and Parent Journey yield such positive shifts in family relationships.

Training the Teen Journey model



Teen Journey is a model for those who seek to incorporate the practices of heart-based rites of passages into their own communities. We have created a structure that allows for communities to bring their own creative ideas and mentorship resources into a program for youth.

If you would like to bring the vision of Teen Journey to your community, we help you lay the foundations for catalyzing this amazing work without reinventing the wheel. Please contact us to explore this opportunity. We look forward to hearing from you as co-creators of an emerging global village connected from the heart.
