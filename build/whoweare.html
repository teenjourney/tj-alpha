Teen Journey empowers young people to lead authentic and connected lives through creativity, self-discovery and modern rites of passage.



We help youth to discover an authentic identity that is rooted in the wisdom of their own hearts-the inner voice that guides them to their unique gifts and expression of who they are. Guided by our local and international leaders, teens learn to trust themselves, develop self-reliance, and learn the joys of living a balanced life.

We are a non-profit organization from Vancouver, Canada that is committed to supporting youth in taking a journey into their hearts to discover who they are through modern rites of passage

OUR MISSION
  • We educate the heart and teach youth the value of self-inquiry
  • We mentor teens in creative ways to create their own authentic identity.
  • We rebuild trust between teens and adults, and grow relationships across generation gaps.
  • We bridge the wisdom of elders with the fresh message of youth.
  • We connect teens to an international community to experience new cultures and perspectives.
  • Act with integrity and responsibility at all times.
  • Trust in the process of self-discovery.
  • Share our gifts and values.
  • To share our gifts and values.
THE PRINCIPLES WE CHERISH AND UPHOLD

What makes us different



Teen Journey takes a deeply rooted and holistic approach the healthy development of a teen transitioning through adolescence.

While many summer programs for youth focus on the outer development of a teen, we help them grow inside and out. We also include the parents in our programming, recognizing that when Teens and Parents grow together the results are far more effective.



Learn about our camps and programs

  • We inquire into who we are as individuals, as an organization, and as a community.

    As an organization, Teen Journey is constantly adapting and growing to meet the needs of our youth and the communities we serve. We practice self-inquiry as a means to ensuring that “who we are” is a reflection of our mission, vision and values.

  • We design rites of passage that are relevant to the modern world we live in.

    We believe that traditional rites of passage are extremely valuable, and must be adapted to meet the needs of the time we live in. Our measure of success is how the youth in our programs are able to relate to themselves and the world in a way that is truly empowering.

  • We create each step of the journey together.

    Teen Journey does not practice a hierarchical model of leadership. We co-create our programs as a team, allowing a powerful synergy to take place between our contributors.

  • We collaborate with local and international elders and mentors who model their message.

    Teen Journey is always refining and selecting elders and mentors that will evolve our organization and bring the best knowledge and experience to our youth. We choose people who model their message, recognizing that what young people seek are living examples to inspire them.

  • We see each teen as possessing a seed of genius that must be recognized in order to grow.

    We believe that genius is neither special nor rare; it is present as a seed within every human being. At Teen Journey, we help youth to discover the seeds of genius already within them so that they seize opportunities to nurture them as they move through life.

  • We are a community.

    All the leaders and mentors on Teen Journey are part of an extended community network. Our continued programming throughout the year allows for teens and families to stay connected to each other and to the mentors that have inspired them.

Our Team



We team with local and international leaders, mentors, and facilitators. These highly-qualified dynamic people are committed to their own and the teens’ unfolding. Chosen precisely because they “walk their talk,” they provide direction and nurture each teen as a whole individual.

Our leaders practice creative mentorship. A creative mentor is a person who can impart knowledge and wisdom in a creative way that will inspire a young person to take risks and make wise choices. Our mentors are here to be living models of our message, meaning that they act from their hearts and have the courage to show up as who they are in the world.

To learn more about creative mentorship: Watch a short video by author Michael Meade, founder of the Mosaic Multicultural Foundation

Many of our leaders provide ongoing support throughout the year, making themselves available to the teens as a calm, safe confidante in times of change and confusion.

Our Story



The vision of Teen Journey came to Ela Rezmer in a remarkable and unexpected call from her heart to create a community where teens could fully explore and discover themselves, their talents and their values. She began gathering people who are passionately committed to themselves and their inner journey; people who also have the desire to share their gifts and wisdom with teens. These mentors help and guide young people through their own personal journey of self-discovery. Without hesitation these leaders came to Teen Journey giving their time, their stories and their inspiration.

Answering the call is a group of volunteers and mentors committed to bridging tribal wisdom with modern culture. Many people have contributed their time and energy to Teen Journey, including people like Malidoma Somé, Dr Vernon Woolfe, Juan Ruiz Naupari of Inkarri and Pneuma – people who are passionate and renowned professionals in their respective fields. As an organization, we already exist in a global village, and are re-connecting with the roots and traditions of the cultures around us to connect the wisdom of sages and scientists. Each of the passionate and committed volunteers and leaders of Teen Journey brings a lifetime of self-exploration and mentorship to our community and its events.

The people of Teen Journey have united around a purpose. Every leader and volunteer has come together around a common cause: to be in service to youth and create wholeness and connection among their families. The underlying purpose and vision of Teen Journey constantly attracts new and remarkable people. Please connect with us if you hear the call.

If you recognize the importance of this work, we call upon you to donate to our organization so that we can reach more youth with our incredible programs!
